B2 Ad Block Detection informs you of the number of browser hits on your WordPress site that have ad blockers

== Description ==

Up to 37% of adults use some form of ad blocking. This can be a big problem for some publishers and/or websites that monetize their assets with ads. 

B2 Ad Block Detection informs site owners of the number of visitors that they are unable to monetize due to ad blockers. 

The B2 Analytics service is free to use. Please agree to our [Terms of Service](https://www.b2.ai/terms-of-service) and [Privacy Policy](https://www.b2.ai/privacy-policy) before deploying this plugin. 

Features include:

*   Overall view of visits to your site with and without ad blockers 
*   Breakdown by day and device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
*   Breakdown by country 
*   7 day & 30 day overview

== Installation ==

1. Simply upload the plugin and activate it! Your authorization key is automatically generated
